Slider

Basic requirements:

1. The slider is infinite, that is, the pictures rotate in a circle in any direction.

2. The slider is adaptive: if 3 pictures fit on the screen, a scheme of 2 rows of 3 pictures is used
if only 2 pictures fit on the screen, a scheme of 3 rows of 2 pictures is used. The mobile version has 3 rows of one picture.

3. Pictures are placed symmetrically depending on the screen resolution.

4. When changing the position of 2 rows of 3 pictures to 3 rows of 2 pictures, all 6 pictures remain on the screen, changing only the order.
